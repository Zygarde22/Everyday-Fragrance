About Everyday Fragrance

Everyday Fragrance is your go-to destination for high-quality fragrances and body washes that elevate your daily routine. Whether you're looking for a fresh, invigorating scent or a soothing, calming aroma, we have something for everyone.

Founded in New Orleans, we draw inspiration from the vibrant culture and rich history of our city. Our mission is to provide you with exceptional products that enhance your well-being and help you express your unique identity.
We believe that fragrance is a powerful form of self-expression, and we are committed to helping you find the perfect scent that resonates with your individuality.

Our Values

At Everyday Fragrance, we are guided by our core values:

  • Quality: We prioritize high-quality ingredients and craftsmanship in all our products.
  • Inclusivity: We celebrate diversity and strive to create products that resonate with everyone.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to environmentally friendly practices and packaging.

Our Founders

Khriston Robinson

Khriston Robinson is the visionary behind Everyday Fragrance. With a background in business and a passion for scents, Khriston founded the company to fill a gap in the market for affordable, high-quality fragrances. His dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction drives the brand forward.

Brenda Stevenson

Brenda Stevenson is the creative force at Everyday Fragrance. With a background in fragrance design and a keen understanding of customer preferences, Brenda plays a crucial role in developing our unique scent profiles. Her artistic vision ensures that our products not only smell amazing but also evoke powerful emotions.
